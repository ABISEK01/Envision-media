FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Do I need prior experience? ▼
No prior experience required! Our courses are designed for complete beginners. We start from the fundamentals and build up to professional-level work.
What is the class schedule? ▼
Classes run Monday to Saturday, 10 AM to 4 PM with a 1-hour lunch break. We also offer weekend batches for working professionals (Saturday-Sunday, 9 AM to 6 PM).
Do you provide certificates? ▼
Yes! Every graduate receives an industry-recognized certificate from Envision Media Works. More importantly, you'll leave with a professional portfolio that speaks louder than any certificate.
Is there placement support? ▼
Absolutely. We have partnerships with studios in Chennai, Bangalore, Hyderabad, and Dubai. Our dedicated placement cell helps with resume building, portfolio reviews, and direct interviews.
Can I pay in installments? ▼
Yes! We offer 3-month EMI options through our partner banks. You can also pay in two installments: 50% at admission and 50% within 30 days.
